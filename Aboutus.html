Vision

Jamtastic in the Philippines aims to enhance stack time and improve the lives of out-of-school youths through high-quality jam production and livelihood initiatives.

Mission

Jamtastic is a company aiming to improve the snack experience for all, regardless of their background, by offering a wider variety of Meryenda flavors..

Objectives

The company aims to offer the highest quality product in the market, prioritizing quality over quantity. -The goal is to offer exceptional customer service to all customers. -The goal is to provide a stable livelihood to out-of-school youths who are willing to work, receive education, and maintain positive attitudes towards potential customers.
